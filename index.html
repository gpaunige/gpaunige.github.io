Our work

Selected case studies

A sample of the engagements we're proud of. Details anonymised where required.

Performance Analytics 2024

Revenue attribution overhaul for a pan-European retailer

Retail & Consumer Goods · 3,200 employees

The client's legacy attribution model was misallocating €40M in marketing spend annually. We rebuilt their measurement framework from the ground up — unifying five data sources into a single source of truth and deploying a multi-touch attribution model tailored to their omnichannel journey.

Outcome 23% improvement in marketing ROI within two quarters of implementation.
Strategic Intelligence 2024

Competitive landscape analysis for a public sector health authority

Public Health · National agency

Ahead of a major procurement decision, we delivered a structured competitive intelligence programme — mapping 14 vendors across capability, pricing, and delivery track record. The analysis surfaced three previously unconsidered providers that changed the shortlist entirely.

Outcome Estimated £2.1M in contract savings; decision made with full market visibility.
Forecasting & Modelling 2023

Demand forecasting engine for a logistics firm scaling into new markets

Logistics & Supply Chain · Series C scale-up

Operating with spreadsheet-based forecasting, the client faced chronic over- and under-stocking across four new geographies. We designed a probabilistic demand model integrated directly into their ERP, with confidence intervals surfaced for non-technical ops leads.

Outcome Forecast accuracy improved from 61% to 89%; inventory costs reduced by 31%.
Organisational Diagnostics 2023

Analytics capability review for a financial services group post-merger

Financial Services · 600 employees

Following a merger, two data teams operated with incompatible tooling, processes, and cultures. Our diagnostic produced a consolidated capability map, identified redundancies, and delivered a 90-day integration roadmap accepted in full by the board.

Outcome Teams merged within 11 weeks; annual tooling costs cut by 40%.
