Data Analysis 2025

Website Engagement Analysis — Session Depth & Visitor Behaviour

Analytics Consulting · GSEM, University of Geneva · Prof. Diego Kuonen

An exploratory analysis of 50,000 website sessions recorded over a single day, examining how deeply visitors browse and identifying patterns in user behaviour. The study revealed that over half of all visits involve only one or two pages, establishing early retention as the primary engagement challenge. Two clearly distinct visitor types were identified — bounce users who exit immediately, and an engaged segment whose median session reaches four pages once past the first. Automated traffic (sessions exceeding 100 pages) was flagged and excluded to ensure analytical integrity.

36.5% Bounce rate — visitors leaving after one page
2 pages Median session length across all 50,000 visits
4 pages Median for engaged visitors (excluding single-page sessions)
Key recommendation Strengthen the landing page experience and introduce clearer calls-to-action to convert bounce visitors into engaged browsers.
Team A. Y. Baccar · G. Bonetta · M. Brunner · Z. Ghafghazi · R. Karimian · A. Markous · S. Ropelewski
